Deja Vue helps you quickly find any video you've shot. Instead of scrolling through thumbnails searching for a long lost video, simply type in the name of the friend you video taped, words that were said or even colors you remember seeing. The tagging is already done for you by Deja Vue.

We do this by using voice and image recognition APIs to tag your videos and photos. Friends' faces are machine recognized, their words captioned, and even simple objects identified to provide search terms. Think of it as a personal search engine for all your videos.
